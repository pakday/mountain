We live and work in these communities just like you, and we want to work together to make them all stronger.
If you’re like us you want to be part of a successful business in a thriving community, but it can be hard to know where to turn to find the connections and resources you need to achieve business success. You’ve likely wondered why it seems so hard to succeed when you know you’re offering something that would benefit those around you in the community.

At the Mountain West Chamber of Commerce, we get it.
We’ve been working in Riverton, Herriman, and Bluffdale since 1997 to build bridges between businesses and the community, and we’ve learned what it takes to build a successful business in our communities. To set you on the path to success we focus on networking, business education, and political involvement within our cities.

We are passionate about providing businesses with the connections, tools, and resources they need to succeed and empowering them to give back to the community. Our community give-back events include an annual teacher appreciation lunch, honoring local civic heroes and business champions, facilitating a weekly legislative roundup (during the legislative session), and supporting and volunteering at city and community events.

Our vision statement says it all: Our members bridge business and community, through leadership and volunteerism. Together we are stronger.
