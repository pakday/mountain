## Primary objectives of the website

- Position the Chamber as a voluntary, non-profit organization of local businesses that promotes collective interests and economic prosperity.
- Showcase networking opportunities, advocacy, and business-friendly policy work.
- Highlight community support, member engagement, and local business growth.

## Goals

- Strengthen the Chamber brand and messaging
- Promote member businesses more effectively
- Increase reach across digital and community channels

## Target market

- Local businesses in Riverton, Herriman & Bluffdale Utah
- Entrepreneurs and corporations seeking networking, advocacy, and business growth
- Small local startups through larger corporations
- Community leaders, non-profits, and professionals enhancing visibility and credibility
