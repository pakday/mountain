WHY BECOME A MEMBER
Looking for a way to grow your business, increase your revenue, build lasting relationships, and give back to the community in meaningful ways? The Mountain West Chamber of Commerce is here to help!

We’re your bridge between businesses and the community in Bluffdale, Herriman, and Riverton, and membership offers you a variety of opportunities and resources.

how we help your business

Build Relationships

Get to know the other businesses in our area through regular networking events, our ambassador program, ribbon cuttings, and more. Your Chamber membership also includes a free membership to our Women in Business program to offer double the opportunities.

Up-level Your Skills

Learn more about a variety of business topics ranging from finance to cybersecurity to HR to marketing (and more!) in our monthly Connect & Learn and Women in Business lunch events.

Gain Visibility

Bring in foot traffic and increase awareness of your business within the community through a ribbon cutting at your brick-and-mortar location. Chamber members are also promoted on our website and social media accounts, and we’re happy to announce any of your events.

Influence Community Decisions

As a part of the Western Growth Coalition, Chamber members have the opportunity to attend and participate in monthly meetings with local chambers, city officials, and businesses to discuss concerns for the west side of the valley. You’re also invited to attend our Legislative Roundup to meet with local legislators to ask questions and express concerns.

Give Back to the Community

Support the community as a whole by participating in our three annual give-back events: Teacher Appreciation Luncheon (honoring 25 local teachers), Scholarship Golf Tournament (funding scholarships for deserving local high school students), and Knight of Heroes (honoring police, fire, healthcare, and business heroes).

Secure Certificates Origin

Do you ship internationally? Certificates of Origin are necessary for most international orders. We sign these for our members. Please make an appointment to schedule.

Our mission is your success, and we’re here to help you grow and succeed within the community!
